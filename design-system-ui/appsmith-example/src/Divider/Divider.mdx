## Usage

- Dividers are a useful tool for organizing content into a distinct groups. They can differentiate between types of information or create visual hierarchy
- Supports horizontal and vertical scrolling, and grows to the length of the parent.
- User dividers sparingly and consider other design elements, such as space, to achieve balance in your design.
